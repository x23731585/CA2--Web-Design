About Us

So, here's the scoop. We're a lively bunch of food enthusiasts with a soft spot for cooking shows. One night, we decided to host a pizza-making extravaganza, with our beloved Food Network humming in the background. And then, in one of those rare, perfect moments of silence (you know the ones... when everyone's too busy stuffing their faces to chat?), Mary Berry's voice breaks through, dropping a shell-shocker on us. "Clams," she says, "are a sustainable seafood alternative." We're all thinking, "Clams? Really? The little shell guys?" But, lo and behold, they're not only sustainable, but they're also little environmental cleaners! We were floored, and now we're on a mission to spread the word. Because who knew, right? Clams, the unsung heroes of the sea. Go figure!
